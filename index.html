About Deepu

College Sweethearts! UTSA
October, 2006

How We Met

Autumn and Ash met in the Fall of 2006 during their Freshman year at UTSA. Autumn was on the dance team, and Ash happened to know a fellow dancer whom he asked to "put in a good word".

After a few days of hearing about "this guy named Ash" who wanted to meet her and stalking him on Facebook, Autumn finally crossed paths with him on campus at the Roadrunner Cafe. They locked eyes, did a double take, and in all honesty it was slightly awkward. It was love at first sight and they both knew it. They went on their first date a few days later.

They Had A Blast! Seeing Little Miss Sunshine At The Movies
October, 2006

Our First Date

Autumn and Ash had a classic first date, the movies. They met on campus and went with some of Ash's friends, two of which are now groomsmen. The movie was Little Miss Sunshine, which was a perfect comedy for the occasion. They had their first kiss that night, and went to the movies again the next day. They were inseparable from that point on, and needless to say, saw a lot of movies that fall.

He Put A Ring On It! San Antonio
December 8th, 2016

How He Proposed

After almost 7 years of dating, Ash proposed the night before Autumn's birthday last year. They were in San Antonio for a wedding that weekend, and had a birthday dinner with her family on Thursday evening. After they left the restaurant, they went to Autumn's parents house for cake and gifts. When it came time to sing "Happy Birthday", Autumn's mom came out of the kitchen with the cake...but this wasn't just any ordinary cake. The first thing Autumn noticed was the cake topper, which was the silhouette of a man proposing to a woman with an ornament in the shape of a ring box next to it. It took a second for her to realize what was going on, but the next thing she knew, Ash was on one knee asking her to marry him while her family finished singing the birthday song in the background. After a couple tears and expressed profanity due to the shock of it all, Autumn said yes.

So Excited! Houston
Present Day

Us Today

Autumn and Ash currently live in Dickinson, a city outside of Houston and close to the water. They are both in real estate, and moved into their own house earlier this year. They love going to Galveston, trying new restaurants in the area, and hanging out with their fur baby, Phillip, "Lip", the cat. After 7 years of living, learning, and loving together, they are ready to become the husband and wife they've always known they were meant to be.
